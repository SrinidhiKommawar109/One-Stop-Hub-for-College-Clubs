Know about NSS

The National Service Scheme plays a vital role in shaping the personality and character of young individuals by involving them in meaningful community service. Through its diverse range of activities and events, NSS not only contributes to the welfare of society but also prepares students to be responsible and empathetic citizens. The smallest act of kindness by an NSS volunteer can indeed make a significant difference, emphasizing the power of collective effort in bringing about positive social change. We aim to develop one's personality through community service. We believe the smallest act of kindness can go a long way.

Events conducted by nss club are:

1.Health and Hygiene companies
2.Environmental activities
3.Educational and Literacy Programs
4.Community Development
5. Cultural Programs
6.Emergency Relief Activities
7.Celebration of Important Days
8.Sports and Recreation
